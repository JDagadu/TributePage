His life in years

  • 1985 - Born on a military base in Germany and raised in Fayetteville, North Carolina.
  • 1999-2008 - Worked hard on his craft, nicknamed Therapist and traveled to Roc the Mic Studio hoping to play it for Jay Z.
  • 2009-2010 - Cole realeased his second mixtape, The Warm Up. He also appeared on Jay Z's album The Blueprint 3. He released his third official mixtape titled Friday Night Lights.
  • 2011-2012 - Produced for Kendrick Lamar. Cole released the third of the "Simba" series. He dropped his debut Album, Cole World: The Sideline Story.
  • 2013 - Born Sinner. That's it that's the tweet.
  • 2014-2017 - I've got two albums for you; Forest Hills Drive and 4 Your Eyez Only. Today marks the annivesary for both albums.
  • 2018 - J Cole was transformed by his journey and his album to tackle drugs was the height of that transformation. KOD.
  • 2019 - Dreamville provided a space (an atmosphere) for selected artistes to collaborate on a project, 2019's Revenge of the Dreamers III.
  • 2020 - Who else but Cole can give a fitting end to a year that we wish to forget. We await The Fall Off. The end?
